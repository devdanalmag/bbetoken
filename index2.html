How It Works

Simple steps to connect projects with skilled jobbers through decentralized smart contracts.

1

Project Posts Job

Projects create detailed job listings and deposit $BBE as escrow into a smart contract.

2

Jobbers Apply

Skilled professionals apply with private profiles showcasing their on-chain proof-of-work.

3

Smart Matching

Our algorithm suggests the most qualified jobbers based on skills, experience, and reputation.

4

Contract Execution

A customized smart contract is deployed with automated payment release conditions.

5

Payment Release

Payments are released per agreed schedule with platform fees automatically deducted.

6

Reputation Update

Both parties rate each other, updating their permanent on-chain reputation scores.
